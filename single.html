About Skyline Residency

Experience the pinnacle of luxury living at Skyline Residency, where modern architecture meets unparalleled comfort. Nestled in the heart of Downtown, this premium development offers spacious 2 & 3 BHK residences designed for discerning homeowners.

With over 70% open green spaces, Skyline Residency provides a serene escape from the city's hustle while keeping you connected to major business hubs, entertainment zones, and educational institutions. Each home is meticulously crafted with Italian marble flooring, floor-to-ceiling windows, and smart home automation features.
